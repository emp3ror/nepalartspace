# ART walk: around Swoyambhu

**Morning sketch and study walk for fine-arts lovers**

*   **Date:** Tuesday, Feb 10, 2026
*   **Time:** 7:00 AM – 11:00 AM
*   **Meeting Point:** Eastern gate of Swoyambhu Stupa
*   **What to bring:** Sketchbook, pencils/inks, water bottle, light snacks

---

## 🎨 Overview

We will circle the Swoyambhu hill together, pausing at vantage points to capture the skyline, stupas, prayer wheels, and morning light. Short prompts will be given to push composition, value studies, and quick ink gestural sketches.

This walk is ideal for fine-arts students, urban sketchers, and anyone wanting to draw outdoors with community.

### What we will cover
1.  Warm-up line exercises and blind contours
2.  Quick compositional thumbnails of the stupa
3.  Perspective notes around the prayer wheel corridor
4.  Ink and wash studies of city views from the hilltop
