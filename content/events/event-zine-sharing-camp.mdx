# Zine Sharing Camp

Bring your zines, mini-comics, risograph tests, and sketchbook pages. We will set up a long sharing table where everyone can trade, gift, and showcase small-run prints.

## What to expect

- Quick demo: one-sheet folded zine (no staples)
- Mini critique circle for anyone who wants feedback
- Swap time with name labels and table sections
- Resource board for local print shops and paper suppliers

## Materials

We will provide A4 paper, markers, glue sticks, scissors, and washi tape. Feel free to bring your own specialty paper or pre-printed spreads.

## Schedule

| Time | Activity |
| :--- | :--- |
| 13:00 – 13:20 | Welcome and setup |
| 13:20 – 14:00 | Folding demo + quick make session |
| 14:00 – 15:30 | Open sharing and swaps |
